Basswood-Prime Plantation Shutters

The trade standard. Consistent and versatile.

Basswood-Prime is our most popular series, and for good reason. Basswood offers a fine, even grain with a consistently smooth surface that accepts paint and stain reliably. Available in a wide range of custom colours and three louver sizes, it is the most versatile option in the VERA range. Batch-to-batch consistency makes it a dependable choice for trade professionals.
